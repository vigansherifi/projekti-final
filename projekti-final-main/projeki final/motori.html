Yamaha MT-07

Overview

The Yamaha MT-07 is a popular middleweight naked motorcycle known for its agile handling, lightweight design, and torque-rich CP2 engine, which delivers strong performance in the low- to mid-range RPMs.

It features an updated chassis with inverted forks for improved handling and a new minimalist aesthetic. Recent models offer a TFT display with smartphone connectivity via the Y-Connect app, and an optional automatic transmission system called Y-AMT is available, which also adds cruise control

This bike is perfect for riders who want to cover more trail in less time and value performance without breaking the bank.
