Super73-ZX

Overview

The Super73-ZX is an electric bicycle that bridges the gap between the entry-level Z-series and the higher-end S-series models. It features a lightweight aluminum frame, a removable battery, and a more comfortable riding position. The ZX is also app-compatible, allowing for customization and firmware updates.
