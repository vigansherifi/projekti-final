Trek X-Caliber 9

Overview

The Trek X-Caliber 9 is a top-tier cross country mountain bike designed for speed and efficiency on singletrack. It's built with a lightweight Alpha Gold Aluminum frame and features a 100mm RockShox Judy Silver fork, SRAM SX Eagle 12-speed drivetrain, and hydraulic disc brakes.

This bike is perfect for riders who want to cover more trail in less time and value performance without breaking the bank.

Specifications

Frame Alpha Gold Aluminum
Fork RockShox Judy Silver, 100mm travel
Shifters SRAM SX Eagle, 12-speed
Brakes Shimano MT200 hydraulic disc
Wheels Bontrager Kovee, Tubeless Ready
Tires Bontrager XR2, 29x2.20"
Weight ~13.5 kg (29.7 lbs)
